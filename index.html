Synapse Summit 2025

The Synapse Summit 2025 showcased various AI-powered innovations addressing different challenges. Highlights included firearm detection systems from ZeroEyes and AccelEye, designed to identify weapons via CCTV to prevent mass shootings. In response to flooding from Hurricane Milton, flood sensors and prevention technologies were prominent due to increased flooding risks and insurance issues. The expo featured a range of AI tools, including virtual fitness trainers, business automation, grant writing, and marketing, reflecting the event's focus on practical AI applications rather than futuristic gadgets.
